Features

Every staging capability, end to end

Six of these are running in the pipeline today — the other two are roadmap items the architecture is already built to support.

◎
Live

Multi-angle consistency

Place a piece once and it projects correctly into every camera angle of the room, using real camera poses recovered from your room scan.

✦
Live

Individual furniture editing

Swap pieces, nudge placement, and adjust color or material on the floor plan — every edit is yours, nothing is locked behind a black-box algorithm.

▦

Floor plan generation

A 2D floor plan with accurate dimensions and camera positions is generated automatically from your room's spatial scan.

▤

AI furniture curation

⬡

One-link 3D asset pipeline

▣
Live

Browser-based compositor

A Three.js renderer projects every staged piece into every photo at the correct perspective — no round trip to external render farms.

Pipeline

From product link to staged photo

Four stages, each usable on its own — built up over time, one room and one piece of furniture at a time.

01

Capture the room

Run room photos through image-blaster (World Labs) to recover camera poses and a floor polygon for the space.

spatialParser.js
02

Build your library

Paste a product link into the Library Builder — CoStage scrapes it, generates multi-angle views, and reconstructs a 3D model.

libraryBuilder.html · assetPipeline.js
03

Place furniture

Drag pieces from your library onto the auto-generated floor plan and export placements with world coordinates.

floorPlanEditor.html
04

Render every angle

The compositor projects each piece into every room photo at the correct perspective — download the staged set.

compositor.html
